    • #what is Chi Hacks?


    Chi Hacks is a 12-hour hackathon for high-schoolers. Students from across the Chicagoland area will come for the weekend, teaming up (or going solo) to build an app, game, or website. We’ll provide free meals & drinks. In the evening, judges will pick the best projects to demo for everyone and win prizes. You’ll meet people, create amazing new things, & most importantly, have fun.
